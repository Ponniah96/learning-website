Introduction

Azure AI-900, also known as Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals, is an entry-level certification that validates foundational knowledge of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) concepts and services within the Microsoft Azure cloud platform. This certification is designed for individuals who want to demonstrate their understanding of AI and ML principles, as well as their ability to work with Azure AI services.

The Azure AI-900 certification is ideal for individuals who are new to AI and cloud computing, including students, professionals looking to transition into AI roles, and those interested in gaining a foundational understanding of AI technologies.
